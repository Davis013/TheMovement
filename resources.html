Find Professional Help

Reaching out to a mental health professional can be a critical step in your wellness journey. But finding the right therapist or counselor for your needs can feel overwhelming.

We've partnered with Pyschology Today, an online database that allows you to search for licensed professionals in your local area. Just enter your location and you'll get matched with counselors, psychiatrists, treatment centers, support groups, and therapists who specialize in treating conditions like yours.

From cognitive behavioral therapy to medication management and beyond, the right mental health expert can provide personalized care to help you work through your mental health struggles. With Pyschology Today, take control of your treatment options by browsing profiles and online scheduling to find the perfect fit.

It takes courage to ask for help. But you deserve to feel heard, understood, and supported. Let Pyschology Today take the guesswork out of getting professional mental healthcare, so you can focus on your journey to wellness.

Help Prevent Suicide

Experiencing suicidal thoughts or urges is a sign of deep distressâ€”but there is hope. If you are struggling with suicidal ideation or know someone who is, immediate support is available right now through the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline.

The Lifeline provides 24/7, free, and confidential support for people in suicidal crisis or emotional distress. You will be connected with a trained crisis counselor who will listen with compassion, offer support, and work to get you any additional help or resources you may need.

You are not alone and help is here. By reaching out to the Lifeline, you've already taken the first step towards feeling better. The crisis counselors understand what you're going through and can help you get to a healthy place, where you feel hopeful again.

If you need someone to talk to: the Lifeline is just a phone call away at 988 or by online chat.
