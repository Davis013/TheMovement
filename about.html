Our Vision

The Movement started as a small community of friends seeking understanding and support around mental health struggles. We realized just how many people feel alone and lost when facing issues like depression, anxiety, trauma, or any mental illness. Too often shame, judgment, and lack of access prevent people from getting the help they deserve.

We envision a world where no one feels isolated in their pain. Where asking for help is met with care, not condemnation. Our dream is for mental wellness to be prioritized in every community, workplace, school, and home.

Who We Are

The Movement is run by a diverse team of mental health advocates. We each have our own experiences struggling with mental illness and wanting change. We know firsthand the power of being vulnerable, finding understanding listeners, and gaining tools to better care for ourselves.

Our small organization is 100% dedicated to providing community support, mental health resources, and stigma-fighting education. We partner with local nonprofits, counselors, support groups, and activists to expand our reach.

Though we come from different backgrounds, we share a core belief: With compassion and courage, we can create a culture of understanding around mental health.
